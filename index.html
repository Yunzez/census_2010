This data visualization is based on census data 2010 washington, the visualization is divided into three parts: race, income and age
The race section concludes the racial distribution of different counties, the race is divided in white, asian, black, native american, and other(including hispanic). The bar graph shows population based on races
The Income section shows the median family income for different counties, with its poverty rank, that is, the rank of poverty rate of the county among all counties in Washington state, from the highest to the lowest
The Age sections shows the age distribution of the county selected, the age is seperated in 9 subcatogory and it shows the age structure of the society of that county
